# User Personas

## 1. Student

**Profile:** Candidate preparing for interviews, career switch, or technical screening.

**Goals:**

- Book a relevant mock interview quickly
- Receive feedback and improvement recommendations
- Build confidence through repetition and structured coaching

**Pain Points:**

- Hard to find the right coach
- Scheduling conflicts
- Unclear feedback after practice sessions

## 2. Tutor

**Profile:** Interviewer, coach, or domain expert offering paid sessions.

**Goals:**

- Monetize expertise
- Manage availability efficiently
- Receive timely payouts and transparent earnings

**Pain Points:**

- Manual scheduling overhead
- Late cancellations and payment disputes
- Limited visibility into earnings and session analytics

## 3. Organization

**Profile:** Company, bootcamp, or institution running structured interview programs.

**Goals:**

- Manage candidates and interview programs centrally
- Track session outcomes and performance reports
- Standardize reviewer workflows

**Pain Points:**

- Fragmented candidate tracking
- Weak reporting and audit trails
- Difficulty scaling across teams or campuses

## 4. Admin

**Profile:** Internal operator responsible for moderation and platform health.

**Goals:**

- Manage users, bookings, disputes, and content
- Control system configuration and categories
- Monitor revenue, incidents, and abuse signals

## 5. Super Admin

**Profile:** Platform owner or executive operator.

**Goals:**

- Set platform-wide policies and access
- Manage security, compliance, and billing settings
- Review strategic metrics and account health
