# Product Overview

## 1. Product Vision

AI Mock Interview & Tutor Booking Marketplace is a software platform for interview preparation, live coaching, booking management, and AI-assisted performance evaluation. The platform enables students to discover tutors, book sessions, pay securely, join live sessions, and receive actionable improvement insights.

## 2. Problem Statement

Current interview preparation is fragmented across tutoring marketplaces, video conferencing tools, scheduling apps, payment gateways, and assessment tools. Users must manually coordinate availability, payments, reminders, and feedback. Organizations also need a structured way to run candidate interview programs at scale.

## 3. Solution Summary

The product unifies the workflow into one platform:

- Tutor availability publishing and slot management
- Student discovery, booking, and payment
- Automated reminders and live session access
- AI mock interview scoring and feedback
- Tutor wallet, commissions, and withdrawals
- Organization programs with candidate tracking
- Admin controls, analytics, and compliance tooling

## 4. Core Modules

| Module | Capability |
| --- | --- |
| Marketplace | Search, filter, and book interview slots |
| Scheduling | Availability, calendars, and conflict prevention |
| Video Session | WebRTC-based live interview room and controls |
| AI Evaluation | Speech, structure, confidence, and content scoring |
| Billing | Subscriptions, commissions, invoices, and refunds |
| Wallet | Tutor earnings, balances, and payout requests |
| Notifications | Email, SMS, push, WhatsApp, and in-app alerts |
| Admin Console | Account operations, disputes, approvals, analytics |

## 5. Primary Users

- Students preparing for technical, behavioral, and language interviews
- Tutors and interviewers monetizing expertise
- Organizations running structured interview or hiring programs
- Platform administrators managing operations and revenue

## 6. Business Outcomes

- Higher conversion through a one-stop interview preparation journey
- Faster session booking and fewer scheduling conflicts
- Revenue through commissions, subscriptions, and enterprise plans
- Measurable learner progress through AI feedback and reports

## 7. Success Metrics

- Booking conversion rate
- Session completion rate
- Tutor activation and retention
- Monthly recurring revenue
- Gross merchandise value processed
- NPS and post-session satisfaction
