# Product Requirement Document

## 1. Objective

Deliver a market-ready software platform that connects students with interview coaches, supports paid bookings, and introduces AI-assisted feedback as a differentiator.

## 2. Product Goals

- Reduce friction in interview preparation
- Convert session demand into repeatable software revenue
- Build trust through secure payments, scheduling, and auditability
- Enable enterprise expansion through organization controls and analytics

## 3. MVP Scope

### In Scope

- User registration, login, and MFA
- Tutor profiles and slot creation
- Search, filter, and booking flow
- Payments and booking confirmation
- Notifications and reminders
- Basic tutor wallet and commission logic
- Admin console with core moderation tools

### Out of Scope for MVP

- Advanced AI scoring engine
- Complex enterprise SSO
- Deep HR workflow automation
- Multi-region active-active deployment

## 4. Product Principles

- Keep booking friction low
- Make pricing transparent
- Design for account and organization growth from day one
- Instrument every revenue and funnel step

## 5. Launch Success Criteria

- Successful booking completion across all supported payment flows
- Reminder delivery accuracy above agreed thresholds
- Stable moderation and payout operations
- Positive tutor and student satisfaction scores
